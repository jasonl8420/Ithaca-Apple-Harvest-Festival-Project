Come join us for our three eventful days of celebrating our local apple harvesters with food, fun, and lots of apples! First time participants of the festival will be exposed to the great atmosphere of a tightly-knitted community in the epicenter of Ithaca.

The Apple Harvest Festival will have diverse farm stands full of fresh, locally grown apples, ranging in large varities of apples to try. Other unique apple varities such as ciders, cider donuts, baked goods and many other goods will also be provided by our amazing local businesses. Along with mouth watering apple goods, there will also be a number of delicious food trucks and other eateries available to smell and taste. Last but certainly not least, numerous talented local performers/entertainers will also make an appearance throughout the festival who will make the festival all that more enjoyable to see and listen.

Health and Safety Policy
  • All attendees are strongly encouraged to be vaccinated and to wear masks in any areas where crowds make it difficult to maintain social distancing of 6 feet between parties.
  • All of our vendors are encouraged to be fully vaccinated and are required to wear masks at all times while they are on the festival grounds and interacting with the public.
  • Regardless of vaccination status, masks are required at the Family Fun Zone happening indoors in Center Ithaca for those 2 years and older and when visiting any stores indoors.
