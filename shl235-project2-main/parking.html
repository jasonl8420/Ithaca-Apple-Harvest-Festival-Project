Parking & Transportations

The Apple Harvest Festival also offers various convenient ways to arrive to the festivals. Check below for further information regarding parking and transportations

Street Parking

Street parking on Monday to Friday is $1.50/hour until 6pm. The parking is free on the weekends and after 6pm on the weekdays. Parking can be paid using both the Paystation Kiosks located on each block or by downloading the Park Mobile App.

Parking Lot

Visitors can utilize Seneca and Cayuga Street Garage that are approximately two minute walk away from Ithaca Commons. Parking is $1.00/hour, but has free parking on Saturday and Sunday of the festival.

Public Transportations

Don't want to deal with the hassle of finding parking spots? Don't worry! Ride TCAT from any of the bus stops to downtown. Green Street and Seneca Street bus stations are part of many major TCAT routes. As an alternative, utilize Ithaca Park n' Ride spots located in the main lot of Wegmans and ride TCAT downtown.
