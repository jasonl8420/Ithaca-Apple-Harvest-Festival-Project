Eateries & Fresh Produce

All along stretch of Ithaca Commons, there is not a place without the inviting smells of all the delicious foods available. In some parts of the Commons, aroma of freshly-picked apples, seasonal baked goods, and many more fill the air. There are also wines, ciders, and other drinks to taste and experience the fresh, locally sourced fruits in different ways. Not to mention a variety of savory and mouthwatering festival foods, ranging from crisp fried chicken with mac and cheese to hotdogs and vegetables on a flat top grill, will also be provided by local food vendors for feast!

Here is a great look at not only one of the many vendors available during the festival but also the bustling atmosphere of the festival. There are loads of fresh apples available, harvested by many local business with care and love. Not to mention there is also a great variety of different apples available to try and explore. Apple ciders are also largely considered a must-try among many locals, adding a new layer of fizz and taste to the base of not just apples, but also to other fruit such as blueberry.

Festival foods provided by local food vendors are also an integral part of the festival that cannot go unnoticed. Now, with the estimated wait time tracker, the waiting is made much easier! From the sizzling grill, variety of tasty dishes will be available, from decliously grilled hotdogs and vegetables to crisp fried chicken with stringy mac n cheese. They are perfect for making a short stop for full stomach before continuing on with the rest of the festival.

To end the meal on a sweet note, the festival will also have a variety of sweets and desserts available! From crispy and sweet kettle corn to the sweet mix of freshly made caramel and freshly harvested apples, the quality provided by Ithaca's local businesses is incomparable.
