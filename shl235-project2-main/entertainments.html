Along with number of delectable eateries and goods available, there are also countless number of entertainments and activities available to please the eyes and ears. Depending on part of the day, there are live performances of local bands, dancers, and more that take place at Bernie Milton Pavilion.

Entertainment Spotlight
Busking

In addition to the list of amazing performances and activities by local bands and groups, another hidden treasure of the Apple Fest are the street performers. Although they won't be easy to come across as they don't perform on the stage, they can often be found scattered across the festival playing style of music unique to each of their own. It is a great way to not only heighten the experience at the festival, but also foster interactions with local performers and other participants as a group, same way other performances and activities do.

Activities

Some activity groups such as GIAC Jumpers will also be participating in the festival. Composed of kids showcasing their passion for not only musical and dance performances, but also conveying a social justice message, GIAC Jumpers also invites other kids to participate, fostering lively atmosphere that is enjoyable for both adults and kids.

The festival also offers numerous festival activities that allow participants of any age to come and enjoy with other visitors of the festival!
